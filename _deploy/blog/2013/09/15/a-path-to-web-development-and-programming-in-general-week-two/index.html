A Path to Web Development & Programming in General: WEEK TWO

Week one was good. A lot of enthusiasm could be seen from you guys. Even though things broke, computers, softwares, lack of coordination etc, the meet-up was quite good. The biggest take away from week one is that, first you crawl and then you walk, things will improve as we go on.


The goal of this week is to code your resume and host it online.


By the end of the week you will be familiar with:

  • Wireframing
  • Twitters Bootstrap Framework &
  • Hosting a Static Website

I will cover the above aspects in a tutorial post within a couple of days. Till then, we all have decided to continue playing around with HTML5 & CSS3 until Wednesday to get our basics right. So for now, complete the Codecedemy’s Web Fundamental track and the Netuts HTML & CSS. Get on with it!
